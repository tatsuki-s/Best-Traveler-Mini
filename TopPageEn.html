Welcome to "BEST TRAVELER."

Background of this site's establishment

 Suddenly, have you ridden a bus since you came to Japan?
 In recent years, the number of public transportation services in rural areas has been decreasing year by year due to the shortage of drivers and other factors.
 However, the loss of buses is expected to cause a variety of problems.
 Elderly drivers may be forced to drive cars even if they are overworked, and traffic accidents may occur.
 Tourists may give up sightseeing because of lack of transportation.
 "BEST TRAVELER." was developed to solve these problems.
